About Jody Donate to the Ocean Conservancy


Thank you for visiting these windows in to my favorite place: the undersea. As a master scuba diver, I cherish the world under the oceans, seas, and all bodies of water. I hope that, in sharing my photos of the extraordinary life of marine ecosystems that people will become as inspired as I am about cleaning our oceans and sustaining efforts toward stewardship of the health of marine ecosystems. If my photos inspire you to help, please donate to the Ocean Conservancy on this page. Bon voyage!
