About Caribbean Diving





Warm-water diving in the Caribbean in places like Cozumel, provides divers with the "80-80-80" experience. This means that the water and air are at least 80 degree fahrenheit, and the visibility underwater is at least 80 feet. These are the most ideal conditions for diving.







Caribbean diving is safest between December-July, as hurricane season generally occurs between August-November. A diver can escape the "Hurricane Belt" by diving in Bonaire and Curacao in the Dutch West Indies. Bonaire is one of my favorite places to dive, as the entire island is a marine reserve dedicated to coral regrowth and marine ecosystem preservation.
