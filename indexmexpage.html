About Caribbean Diving

Diving in Cozumel - July 2023

Warm-water diving off the Yucatan Peninsula in Mexico (specifically, in Cozumel in July, 2023 we find what divers call 80-80-80 conditions. This means that the air and water are at least 80 degrees fahrenheit, and the visibility underwater is at least 80 feet, making diving in the Caribbean Sea one of the most optimal places to dive in the world from December-July. Diving there between August-November is risky, as hurricanes often hit diving locations in the Caribbean during those months. However, a diver can escape hurricanes and dive in Bonaire or Curacao in the Dutch West Indies, which is south of the hurricane belt, and experience these conditions. I have found diving in Bonaire to be some of the best in the world, as the island is a marine preserve dedicated to re-growing coral and not allowing cruise ships or even ferries to keep marine ecosystems pristine.
